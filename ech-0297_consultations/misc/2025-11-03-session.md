### Protokoll:
- Identifiers
  - "Old world": Technical identifier + "Geschäftsnummer"
  - "New world": Unique identifier which
    - is uniform for all consultations
    - follows, e.g., the format `ostermundigen.ch/baudirektion/consultation/ABC123` / `admin.ch/eda/consultation/ABC123`
  - It would be great to have the year in the identifier, but to ensure stability, the year should probably be retrieved from the metadata
 - Titles
   - Official titles are often very long – and ignored in communication
   - Should the standard offer both a `title` and `title_short` field?
   - Decision: Only `title`
   - Reasoning: Ideally consultation organisers would stick to shorter titles, max. 150 chars
 - Reviewed metadata
